## App Overview
This app features a quiz with 10 questions about Saudi Arabia. It will help you gauge your knowledge of my country. Correct answers will be highlighted in green, while incorrect answers will be marked in red. After completing the quiz, you'll see a score screen: if you score more than 5 points, congratulations, you have a strong knowledge of Saudi Arabia! If not, there's more to learn.
